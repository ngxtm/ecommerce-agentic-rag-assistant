# Returns Policy

## Overview
This document describes the internal returns policy for orders placed through our e-commerce platform.

## Standard Return Window
- Most items may be returned within 30 calendar days of delivery.
- The return window begins on the confirmed delivery date shown by the carrier.
- Returns requested after 30 calendar days are generally not eligible unless an exception is approved.

## Return Eligibility
An item is eligible for return if:
- it was delivered within the last 30 days
- it is unused or only lightly handled for inspection
- it is returned in the original packaging when possible
- it includes all accessories, manuals, and bundled components

## Non-Returnable Items
The following items are generally not returnable:
- final sale or clearance items
- personalized or custom-made products
- gift cards
- downloadable software
- digital subscriptions
- personal care items that were opened
- items damaged due to misuse by the customer

## Damaged, Defective, or Incorrect Items
If the customer receives a damaged, defective, or incorrect item:
- support should apologize and document the issue
- the return window requirement may be waived if the issue is confirmed
- the customer may be offered a replacement, refund, or return label depending on the case

Customers should report damaged or incorrect items within 7 calendar days of delivery whenever possible.

## Return Method
Eligible returns may be processed through one of the following methods:
- prepaid return label issued by support or self-service return flow
- drop-off at an approved carrier location
- merchant-authorized pickup for oversized items when applicable

## Return Shipping Fees
- Return shipping is free if the item is defective, damaged, or incorrect
- Return shipping may be deducted from the refund for discretionary returns unless a free return promotion applies
- Original outbound shipping charges are generally not refunded unless the return is due to our error

## Exchanges
We do not process direct exchanges in all cases.
When exchange is unavailable:
- the original item should be returned
- a refund is processed after inspection
- the customer may place a new order for the replacement item

## Return Inspection
Returned items are inspected upon receipt.
A return may be rejected or partially refunded if:
- the wrong item was sent back
- major accessories are missing
- the item shows signs of heavy use, misuse, or damage caused after delivery

## Refund After Return
Once the returned item is received and inspected:
- approved refunds are usually initiated within 3 to 5 business days
- the final posting time depends on the customer’s payment provider
- refund timing details are governed by the refund policy

## Late or Missing Returns
If a return label is issued but the package is never handed to the carrier, the return request may expire.
If the carrier shows no movement for several business days, support may open an investigation before issuing a replacement or refund.

## Support Guidance
When answering return questions:
- confirm whether the item is within the 30-day return window
- identify whether the item is standard, damaged, defective, incorrect, or non-returnable
- do not promise an immediate refund before return inspection unless a policy exception applies
- refer to the refund policy for refund timing and payment method rules
