# Refund Policy

## Overview
This document defines the internal refund policy for customer orders and approved returns.

## General Refund Rule
Refunds are issued only after one of the following occurs:
- a return is received and approved after inspection
- a cancellation is confirmed before shipment
- a damaged, defective, or incorrect item case is approved
- a lost-package claim is approved after investigation

## Refund Method
Refunds are issued to the original payment method whenever possible.
If the original payment method cannot accept refunds, support may escalate the case for alternative resolution according to finance procedures.

## Refund Processing Time
- Approved refunds are usually initiated within 3 to 5 business days
- Bank posting times vary by payment method and financial institution
- Credit card refunds may take 5 to 10 business days to appear
- Debit card refunds may take 3 to 10 business days to appear
- Gift card or store credit refunds may appear faster than bank-based refunds

## Shipping Charge Refunds
Original outbound shipping charges are generally not refunded unless:
- the wrong item was shipped
- the item arrived damaged or defective
- the carrier returned the package due to our fulfillment error

Expedited or premium shipping fees are not refunded for voluntary returns.

## Partial Refunds
A partial refund may be issued when:
- returned items are missing accessories
- the item is returned in materially different condition
- only part of a bundled order is returned
- a promotional discount must be prorated across multiple items

## Cancelled Orders
If an order is cancelled before shipment:
- a full refund is generally issued
- the refund is sent to the original payment method
- the processing timeline still depends on the payment provider

If the order has already entered fulfillment or shipped, standard cancellation may no longer be possible and the return policy will apply instead.

## Returned but Not Yet Refunded
If the customer states that the item was returned but the refund is not visible:
- confirm the carrier shows delivered to the return facility
- allow up to 5 business days for inspection and refund initiation
- if more than 7 business days have passed after confirmed return delivery, escalate for investigation

## Lost or Undeliverable Shipments
A refund may be approved for a package that is:
- confirmed lost by the carrier
- undeliverable and returned to sender
- significantly delayed with no tracking movement after investigation

Support should not promise a lost-package refund until the carrier review or internal investigation is complete.

## Gift Returns
If a gift order is eligible for return:
- the refund may be issued as store credit or gift balance, depending on the payment and gifting workflow
- cash refunds are generally issued only to the original purchaser’s payment method

## Non-Refundable Cases
Refunds are generally not issued for:
- final sale items
- gift cards
- digital products already accessed or downloaded
- items damaged due to misuse
- returns submitted outside the approved window without exception

## Support Guidance
When discussing refunds:
- explain the difference between refund initiation and bank posting time
- avoid guaranteeing exact posting dates
- confirm whether the refund is full or partial
- reference the return policy if the item must be received before refund approval
