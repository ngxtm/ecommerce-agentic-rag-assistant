# Order Tracking FAQ

## What order statuses can a customer see?
Common shipment and fulfillment statuses include:
- Order received
- Processing
- Payment review
- Ready to ship
- Shipped
- Partially shipped
- Out for delivery
- Delivered
- Delayed
- Undeliverable
- Cancelled

## When is tracking information available?
Tracking information is usually available within 24 hours after the order is marked as shipped.
In some cases, carriers need additional time before the first tracking scan appears.

## Why does my order show multiple packages?
Orders may ship from multiple fulfillment centers.
Customers may receive:
- multiple tracking numbers
- separate delivery dates
- partial shipment updates before the rest of the order is dispatched

## Why is my tracking not updating?
Tracking may not update immediately because of:
- carrier scan delays
- weekend or holiday processing
- severe weather
- package handoff between logistics partners

Support should usually advise the customer to wait 24 to 48 hours after shipment before escalation unless the package is past the estimated delivery window.

## Can a customer check order status through chat or support?
Yes. Customers may ask for shipment status through chat or support.

## What verification is required before sharing order status?
Before the assistant or support agent shares order-specific shipment details, the customer must provide:
- full name
- date of birth
- last 4 digits of SSN

This verification step is required to protect customer privacy and order information.

## Can partial verification be accepted?
No. Shipment details should only be shared after all required verification fields are collected and validated.

## What date format should be used for date of birth verification?
For this support workflow, the date of birth should be entered in DD-MM-YYYY format.

## What happens if the provided verification data does not match?
If the verification details do not match the order records:
- do not disclose shipment details
- ask the customer to confirm the information
- if repeated attempts fail, advise the customer to contact live support for further assistance

## Can a customer update the shipping address after shipment?
No. Once an order has shipped, the shipping address cannot be changed through standard support workflows.
If the package has not shipped yet, support may submit a request, but approval is not guaranteed.

## Can support guarantee a delivery date?
No. Support may provide the carrier’s latest estimated delivery date, but should not guarantee delivery unless there is a confirmed service commitment.

## What should support do for delayed packages?
If the package is delayed:
- review the latest tracking event
- compare it with the estimated delivery window
- advise the customer to wait 2 additional business days if the delay is minor
- escalate if there is no movement for 5 or more business days after shipment

## What should support do for delivered-but-not-received claims?
If tracking shows delivered but the customer cannot find the package:
- advise the customer to check with household members, building staff, and nearby delivery locations
- ask the customer to wait until the end of the delivery day
- escalate if the package is still missing after 1 business day

## Can a cancelled order still be tracked?
No. A cancelled order does not receive shipment tracking because it is not fulfilled for delivery.
