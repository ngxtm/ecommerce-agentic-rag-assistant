# Shipping Policy

## Overview
This document describes the standard shipping policy for our e-commerce customer support operations. It is intended for internal use by the customer support assistant and support agents.

## Order Processing Time
- Orders are typically processed within 1 to 2 business days.
- Orders placed after 5:00 PM Eastern Time are treated as received on the next business day.
- Orders are not processed on company-observed holidays.

## Shipping Methods
We currently support the following shipping methods for domestic orders:
- Standard Shipping: estimated delivery in 3 to 7 business days
- Expedited Shipping: estimated delivery in 2 to 3 business days
- Overnight Shipping: estimated delivery in 1 business day for eligible orders

Shipping method availability may vary based on destination, item size, inventory location, and carrier serviceability.

## Free Shipping Eligibility
- Free standard shipping is available for orders of 50 USD or more before tax.
- Free shipping promotions may apply to selected campaigns and eligible products only.
- Free shipping does not apply to oversized items, freight shipments, or international orders unless explicitly stated.

## Delivery Estimates
Delivery estimates are calculated from the shipment date, not the order date.
Transit times are estimates only and are not guaranteed unless explicitly stated in a promotion or service-level agreement.

## Carriers
Orders may be shipped using one of the following carriers:
- UPS
- FedEx
- USPS
- Regional delivery partners

Carrier selection is determined automatically based on package size, destination, and operational efficiency.

## Tracking Information
- Tracking information is usually available within 24 hours after the order is marked as shipped.
- Some carriers may take additional time to activate the tracking number.
- Customers may receive separate tracking numbers if items ship in multiple packages.

## Multiple Shipments
A single order may be fulfilled from multiple warehouses.
As a result:
- items in the same order may ship separately
- customers may receive multiple delivery dates
- the order status may remain partially shipped until all items leave the warehouse

## Shipping Address Changes
Shipping addresses cannot be changed after the order enters fulfillment.
If a customer contacts support before fulfillment begins, support may submit a request to update the address, but changes are not guaranteed.

## Delayed Deliveries
Delivery delays may occur due to:
- severe weather
- carrier disruptions
- high seasonal demand
- payment review
- inventory transfer between fulfillment locations

If a package is delayed beyond the estimated delivery window, support should advise the customer to wait 2 additional business days before escalation unless the package is marked as lost or undeliverable.

## International Shipping
International shipping may be available for selected destinations.
Customers are responsible for:
- import duties
- customs fees
- local taxes
- compliance with local import restrictions

Transit times for international orders vary by destination and customs processing.

## Lost or Undeliverable Packages
A package may be considered potentially lost if:
- tracking has not updated for 5 or more business days after shipment, or
- the estimated delivery date has passed and there is no confirmed delivery event

Packages marked undeliverable by the carrier are usually returned to the fulfillment center. Once the return is received, the customer may be offered a replacement or refund according to the refund policy.

## Support Guidance
When responding to shipping questions:
- provide the estimated delivery window if available
- provide tracking details only if the customer is verified
- avoid guaranteeing delivery dates unless the carrier has confirmed them
- use the latest shipment data from the order status tool when available
